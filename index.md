## 🛠️ Frequently Asked Questions

What if the application freezes?
Close the application by clicking the X in the corner. Reopen it from your desktop. The agent resumes from the last save point.

Does this work while my computer is in sleep mode?
No. The application requires an active internet connection and an awake state to monitor market conditions. Adjust your Windows power settings to keep the screen and connections active while you trade.

How do I remove the software?
Go to your Windows Settings, select Apps, find Vibe-Trading in the list, and click Uninstall. This removes all program files from your system.

What happens if the internet cuts out?
The application notifies you with an alert sound. It pauses all pending orders to prevent errors. Once the connection returns, you must restart the engine manually from the control panel.
